About

Who's behind these, and why they exist.

I'm Karun Bourishetty — a master's student in divinity.

This site is my working notebook: a blog and a running documentation of what I'm learning and coming to understand from the Bible, set down as plainly as I can. I'm drawn to the basic questions — what Christianity actually is, what it claims, how it differs from other religions and worldviews, and what the text says on its own terms.

It's a work in progress, and it will stay that way. As I move further into my studies I'll keep adding to it — new findings, new concepts, and clearer explanations of the ones already here.
